Projects

DHPP
Damless hydroelectric power plant DHPP technology

DHPP is an energy complex that uses a renewable, practically inexhaustible source of energy of water flows, and allows to produce cheap electricity in volumes sufficient not only for certain regions, but even for entire continents, with high economic efficiency and a stable amount of installed generation. At the same time, the BHP located inside the stream at a certain depth does not interfere with navigation, does not destroy the underwater ecosystem, does not distort the landscape of the area, does not pollute the environment. The coefficient of utilization of the installed capacity of BHPS is close to the values of dam hydroelectric power plants and surpasses solar, wind and wave power plants.

APPLICATION
Electricity generation with the help of water flow.

Usumaki
Device For Improving The Adhesion Of Wheel Pairs With Strips

The device proposes a method of creating friction properties of the disc brake, the basis of which is simultaneous use of at least two and more friction materials in the power scheme of interaction of the brake pad (pad) and brake disc taking into account individual load of each of them. Combination of several traditional friction materials in the power scheme of the disc brake allows constructive actions to reach the necessary operational qualities of the disc brake without application (or development of new) more expensive friction materials (which is impossible to achieve due to use of only traditional friction materials on an individual basis).

APPLICATION
Wheeled and rail transport, where the friction brakes are used.
